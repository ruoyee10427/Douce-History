Welcome to Douce History

At Douce History, we craft exquisite cakes that tell sweet stories. Founded in 2020, our bakery combines traditional recipes with innovative flavors to create unforgettable dessert experiences. Each cake is meticulously handmade using the finest ingredients, from rich Belgian chocolate to premium Japanese matcha. Whether it's a special celebration or a simple indulgence, our creations are designed to delight your senses and create beautiful memories. Taste the history of sweetness with Douce History!
